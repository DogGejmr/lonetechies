Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a website?

Typically, a single-page website takes 1-2 weeks, while a multi-page website takes 2-4 weeks. The exact timeline depends on the project complexity and revision rounds.

Do I need to provide my own content?

Yes, you'll need to provide the text content and images for your website. We can guide you on content structure and recommend stock photos if needed.

What's included in the SEO package?

Our SEO package includes meta tags optimization, keyword research, image optimization, responsive design, and proper heading structure to help your site rank better in search engines.

How does the revision process work?

During the development phase, you can request unlimited revisions to ensure the website meets your vision. We'll implement changes until you're completely satisfied with the result.

Do you provide website hosting?

While we don't provide hosting directly, we can recommend reliable hosting providers and help you set up your website on your chosen platform.

What happens after the website is launched?

Depending on your package, you'll receive support for a specified period after launch. During this time, we'll help with any questions or minor adjustments needed.
